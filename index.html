Simple, beautiful, baby.

So you want to keep track of all that stuff your baby does (or that you do for her)? But really, who has the time? Pachyderm is an easy to use, beautiful to look at tool, that lets you track all the important stuff with a few easy taps. Feedings, changings, sleep, and even smiles. And when you want to check in on how things are going, Pachyderm has beatuiful charts and graphs to see what's going well, and help you understand your baby better.

So what does it do?

  • Super fast data entry

    Lets be real, you're a new parent. You barely have time to wipe your own ass, so data tracking should be super simple.

  • Easy Data Export

    Want to slice the data yourself? Pachyderm will let you easily download all your data so you can go to town

  • Input from any channel

    Pro users can set up import channels to send data to pachyderm via Email, SMS, or even gchat!
